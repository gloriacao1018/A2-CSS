Shifting perspective: ‘Inception VR’ makes user question their reality

Author: Riaane Cox

“Inception VR” was inspired by the everyday worker or student who is in need of an escape from monotonous reality. How can we give them a shift in reality that may leave them with new perspectives? The experience begins by putting on a VR headset inside the Digital Narratives seminar room at the Internationale Filmschule Köln. The user enters a 3D, virtually constructed model of the room they are physically standing in.
