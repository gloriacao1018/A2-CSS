MA Digital Narratives is now open for applications!

Author: MA Digital Narratives

The programme offers dedicated media and cultural professionals an opportunity to critically engage and experiment with storytelling and production techniques for digital media, with a special focus on immersive technologies. Throughout the programme, students conceptualise, design, and develop digital narrative projects. Previous and current projects include: interactive web series and films, transmedia theatre productions, mobile games, VR experiences, AI conversations, and interactive installations, among others.
